A JOURNEY FROM CORPORATE LEADERSHIP TO ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP

Mr. Rahul Singh's journey is a testament to the power of purpose-driven transformation. For over two decades, Rahul was a formidable force in the corporate world. Witnessing the escalating environmental challenges, he made the courageous decision in 2018 to step away from his career to found "The Earth Company" and later "The Earth Foundation" in 2020.
